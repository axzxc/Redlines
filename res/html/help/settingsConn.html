Settings - Connections

CID

Displays your node’s unique CID (Connection Identifier). Click it to copy it to your clipboard for sharing or backup purposes.

Port

Shows the port Redlines uses to accept inbound connections from other nodes. Changing this will regenerate your CID, which could impact existing connections.

IP

Shows your device's current IP address. Click to copy it to your clipboard if you need to share your node location with others manually.

Add Node by CID

Paste another node’s CID into the input field to manually add it to your node list. Useful for directly connecting to a known peer.

Import/Export Node List

Import a `.nl` file containing nodes from another Redlines instance, or export your current list to back it up or migrate it to another device.

View Node List

Displays all currently known nodes, including their IP addresses. Lets you manually inspect and optionally test or ping each for availability.

Clear Node List

Deletes your entire list of known nodes. This action is irreversible and may disconnect you from your current network of peers unless restored from backup.
