Redlines - Settings

Account

Displays your local account credentials including your username, and changeable password.

Preferences

Toggle various user preferences such as debugging output, and the ‘Go Public’ option which restricts connections to your local node for privacy and offline use.

Data Management

Shows how much space your topics occupy, lets you configure limits for topic sizes, and manage imports/exports or backups.

Connections

Displays your current network node information, including your own identifier and connected nodes. Allows manual node list updates, CID additions, and importing/exporting node data.
